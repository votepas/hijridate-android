An Android app that displays Hijri Date and Moon Phase.

Download from Google Play: [Islamic Hijri Date](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.mypapit.mobile.mobimoon)

Supported Language:

  * English (International)
  * Indonesian
  * Malay
  * French
